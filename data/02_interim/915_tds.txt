DESCRIPTION
915 sealant is a one-component, smooth polyurethane adhesive
capable of dynamic joint movement totaling 70% of original joint
geometry (±35%). The sealant cures to a tough, flexible rubber
when exposed to moisture present in the atmosphere.
Bostik 915's all-season formula has physical properties that will
remain stable over time. The cured performance temperature range
is -40°F to 150°F. Where textured appearance is needed, please use
Bostik 916.
